    About us

    Veracraft established in 2018, is a quality professional food supplier commissioned after Mr Moleko combined his experience in relevance to the company services. From Modest beginings in Johannesburg area, the company can now cater to the Sourthrn African region. Veracraft is committed to the latest Occupational Health & Safety Act. The company has built a reputation for outstanding service and the highest levels of expertise when it comes to all of your agricultural & construction requirements.
